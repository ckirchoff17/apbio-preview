This workbook previews the eight core units of AP Biology — the concepts, landmark discoveries, key scientists, and AP-style questions that will define your year. Use it this summer to build the mental framework you need to think like a biologist on day one.

🔬
What AP Bio is really about AP Biology is organized around four Big Ideas: evolution, cellular processes, genetics & information transfer, and interactions in biological systems. The exam is 60 multiple-choice questions plus 6 free-response questions — and it heavily emphasizes data analysis, experimental design, and applying concepts to novel scenarios rather than memorizing isolated facts.
